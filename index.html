Executive Summary

Koko is a peer-to-peer marketplace platform with a unique value proposition centered on negotiation-driven commerce — a feature deeply rooted in African cultural trading traditions. With over 2,000 downloads across multiple countries (Nigeria, Indonesia, Pakistan, Nepal, Bangladesh, Sri Lanka, Ghana, and India), the platform has achieved organic international traction.

This proposal outlines a comprehensive 6-month marketing strategy designed to:

  • Establish strong brand identity and positioning
  • Build direct audience engagement beyond influencer dependency
  • Drive daily active user engagement and retention
  • Scale user acquisition across Nigeria and international markets
  • Create sustainable, measurable growth with clear KPIs

6. Referral & Ambassador Program (Special Program)

We propose a dual-layered approach to community growth: a broad Referral Engine for all users and a curated Ambassador Pilot for high-impact advocates.

6.1 The Referral Engine (Viral Growth)

Designed to lower customer acquisition costs (CAC) by incentivizing the existing user base to invite their network.

  • The Mechanic: "Give NGN 500, Get NGN 500" (credited as platform tokens/badges) or priority listing status for sellers who refer others.
  • Gamification: "Top Referrer" leaderboards with monthly prizes to spark competition.
  • Trust Factor: In-app badges like "Trusted Referrer" to enhance the negotiator's profile.

6.2 The Ambassador Pilot (Phased Rollout)

Starting in **Month 3**, we will recruit 10–15 "Koko Champions" in high-density markets (Lagos, Abuja, Port Harcourt) and major university campuses.

  • Recruitment: Focused on campus leaders, micro-influencers, and the most active 1% of Koko users.
  • Primary Tasks: Local "negotiation" workshops, campus demo sessions, and content creation for TikTok/Reels.
  • Performance Tracking: Each ambassador receives a unique trackable link/code to monitor their direct impact on app installs and active listings.
